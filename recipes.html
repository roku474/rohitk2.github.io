Some of My Favorite Recipes

This Jiggly Japanese Cheesecake recipe was one of the first difficult cakes that I attempted. While I did mess up on many of my attempts, it was still delicious everytime. On my best try, it was absolutely amazing and ended up as my favorite recipe. I highly recommend this recipe to anyone looking for a bigger baking challenge with an amazing reward.

This Tres Leches recipe was a very unique experience for me. I had this cake one time while I was on a vacation, and I was very surprised by its texture when I first had it. I asked what it was, and the baker sent me this recipe, so now I am passing it along even further. I found the entire process very novel with how the cake has to be prepared over time. If anyone else wants an entirely new baking experience, I very much recommend this recipe.

This Mirror Glaze recipe was not something I did for the flavor, but rather the aesthetic. As you can see just by the thumbnail, mirror glaze cakes look nothing like their normal counterparts. With a very special way of making this glaze, the outer layer ends up with this shiny surface that just looks gorgeous. If you want to make something quite visually appealing, I think this recipe is perfect for you.
